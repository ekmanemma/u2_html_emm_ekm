Sustainable interior design

Lorem Ipsum is one of the leading interior design studios in Scandinavia working with a wide range of both commercial styling and private clients. The studio was founded in 2008 by Lotta Agaton, Pella Hedeby and Nina Holst.

We have more than 20 years of experience working with interiors, including styling, creative direction and consulting for many international brands. Our main focus and expertis is sustainable, scandinavian and minimalistic interior.

Society is becoming increasingly aware of the importance of sustainable interior design. We feel that as designers we have a responsability to carefully choose materials and products with the lowest enviromental impact possible.

We take on commercial stylist projects, develop interior concepts for property developments and do interiors for both private residences and commercial spaces. Every project is tailor made to suit the needs of the client.
