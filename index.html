Sustainable interior design

We are interior stylists and designers based in Stockholm. We work with interiors and styling for both private customers and companies. Our studio is located in Stockholm but we work all over all over europe.

Our main focus and expertis is sustainable, scandinavian and minimalistic interior and we've gathered many years of experiences from different interior missions and projects. Together we make a great team for all kinds of interior styling.

Society is becoming increasingly aware of the importance of enviromentally responsible building and interior design. We feel that as interior designers we have a responsability to carefully choose materials and products with the lowest enviromental impact possible.

No project is to small or to big, so wether it is to furnish your kitchen or to create a campaign for a new launch or collection, we are here for you! All projects are different but our goal is to make you completely satisfied with the result and you will be a part of the entire design process.
