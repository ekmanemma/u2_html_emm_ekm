Interior stylists

Lotta Agaton

With a background as the co-founder of Residence magazine, Agaton has a deep knowledge of editorial stylist collaborations and the impact of good visual communication. She is known for her minimalistic yet warm style which is always based on sustainable quality and timelessness with a poetic edge.

Her long term passion for interiors has yielded an impressive list och clients such as Iittala, String, Kasthall, Herman Miller, IKEA, H&M among private clients for over 20 years.

Pella Hedeby

Pella has been in the interior business for 15 years and 2016 she was anounced with Elle's Interiorblogger Award of the year. Organic design has always been a part of her inspiration and she's always working with sustainable materials.

And sure, design trends is a big part of this industry, but her focus is on your personal styling and your way of living because that's how you create a sustainable home.

Nina Holst

If you love Scandinavian design then surely you have heard or seen pictures by decorator Nina Holst. Her blog Stylizimo made her the most popular Scandinavian interior blogs, filled with typical minimalistic Scandinavian design. Her long term passion for interior decoration has yielded an impressive list och clients such as Bemz, Granit, IKEA, H&M among private clients for over 20 years.

She has a degree in interior and architectural design, she has a lot of experience in apartment-styling, furnishing for private customers and interior missions for IKEA She has always had a passion for enviromental issues, which is the base in her stylist work today.
